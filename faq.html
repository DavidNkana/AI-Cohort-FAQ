1. Help keep AI Cohort focused- use it to explain the specific sections you're interested in. AI Cohort sometimes explains code suboptimally when you use more code.

2. AI Cohort understands class, variable, and method names. If your names are accurate, AI Cohort will perform better. If your names are vague, try to improve your names. If your names may be inaccurate, try replacing them with vaguer substitutes foo, bar, or _

What languages does AI Cohort support?

1. AI Cohort works with almost all programming languages, including Python, Rust, C, C++, Java, Ruby, JavaScript, C#, Go, CSS, JSX (React), and many more. Rarer languages such as COBOL, AutoHotKey, and V also work well. The best way to find out if your language is supported is to try it and see.
